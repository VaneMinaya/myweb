First Project

Design of mill and press for the production of cocoa

This project is about a machine that my partners and I created in our second cycle of classes at university that removes oil from the seeds of cacao based on the pressure exerted by a hot press at a mill. Well this is what happens with this project. The machine is heated by a resistor in order to soften the seed and seed fat is liquid again.

It is an economical alternative for artisanal mining and you can drive to the small business.

Second Project

Self-Cleaning Clothes.

Preparation of self-cleaning cotton fabrics which employ titanium dioxide nanoparticles obtained by the sol gel method. We researched the sol-gel to obtain TiO2 nanoparticles with cotton garments method being coated.

The objective of this project is to obtain self-cleaning cotton fabrics that remove stains clothing to expose to the sun.

It is concluded that it is possible to obtain TiO2 nanoparticles from the precursor (anatase).
